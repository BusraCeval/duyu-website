ABOUT US

Traditional Manufacturing Industries such as Carpet, Textile or Plastics fail to keep up with and adapt to enterprise-grade innovation and technology; unlike their high tech, high value counterpart-industries such as Oil, Electronics or Transportation. At EkstrAi, our mission is to change that narrative and enable cutting-edge Industrial AI and Industry 4.0 capabilities for Traditional Manufacturing most seamlessly, backed by processes designed specifically for the challenges Traditional Manufacturing faces for wide and rapid adaption rate. We attest by and stay true to our mission, with our first product DUYU. DUYU, leverages Computer Vision and Deep Learning to enable real-time, smart reporting and supervision capabilities in production lines and shop floors in a machine brand/year and industry agnostic manner.
